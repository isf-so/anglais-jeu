Welcome to the Restaurant

You are invited to dine at a typical restaurant in Mulhouse. The cobbled streets and Alsatian architecture transport you to a time when simplicity and elegance were the order of the day. You enter the restaurant, a warm place with red brick walls and carefully set tables. The delicate aroma of regional dishes is already making your mouth water.

Tonight, it's a unique experience. You will discover French culinary culture and experience a true French dinner. But beware, every detail matters in this type of restaurant! Will you be able to follow the rules of etiquette and adopt the proper manners? Your choices will determine the success of your dinner.

Game Rules

  • You will receive a series of questions at each stage of your dinner.
  • Each correct answer earns you 10 points.
  • Each incorrect answer deducts 5 points.
  • Your goal is to finish the dinner with the highest score possible.
  • The objective is to surpass your friend's score!
